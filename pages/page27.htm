To Apply Paint Color:

  • For small, detailed areas, use the paint brush.
  • Select a brush size that suits your needs.
  • For larger, expansive areas, click on the paint bucket.

To Remove Paint Color:

  • Click on the eraser tool to remove color from an area.
  • Select an eraser size that is right for the job.

To Mask Off An Area:

  • If you want to mask (tape off) specific areas of your photo, click on the painter's tape tool.
  • For taping off a straight line, click the straight line tool.
    • To anchor the starting point of your tape, click on your image.
    • Drag your cursor to the end point and click again.
  • For taping off a shape, click the shape tool.
    • To begin, click on the starting point of your shape.
    • Drag your cursor to the next point and click again.
    • Contine dragging and clicking until you have defined your shape.
    • Make sure you close your shape. Click once to finish.
